About Ram Chemical Enterprises

Established in 1999 in Agra, India, Ram Chemical Enterprises has grown to become a trusted and reliable name in the water treatment industry. As an ISO Certified 9001:2015 company, we are dedicated to providing a comprehensive range of Water Treatment Chemicals, Water Treatment Equipment, and Water Treatment Plants. We are manufacturers and dealers of all types of plants and chemicals for wastewater treatment, alongside a robust portfolio of basic chemicals.

Under the visionary leadership of our founder and Technical Director, Mr. Ramesh Chauhan (B.Tech Chemical IIT Roorkee), with decades of industry experience, we have cultivated a legacy of innovation and client trust. Our success is further bolstered by Mrs. Anju Chauhan (B. Tech.
